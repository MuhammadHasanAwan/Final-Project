About Us

Restro, founded by Umesh Seejwani & Muhammad Hasan in 2024, is a global fast food restaurant chain specializing in fried chicken. With a presence in over 145 countries and territories, Restro is known for its signature Colonel's Original Recipe fried chicken, along with other menu items like chicken tenders, sandwiches, and sides. The brand is committed to providing fresh, delicious food with a focus on quality and innovation.
